DETECTMiND

An academic research app for understanding everyday smartphone behaviour and wellbeing — run by the University of Southampton.

Install Instructions → For invited participants only

About the Study

DETECTMiND is part of a research programme investigating the relationship between smartphone usage patterns and mental wellbeing. Participants are invited individually by the research team.

If you have not been personally invited to take part, this app is not intended for you.

What the App Does

Collects sensor data

App usage, notifications, screen state, light level, location, call/SMS counts, gestures.

Asks short surveys

Brief experience-sampling questions delivered at scheduled times throughout the day.

Uploads securely

All data is transmitted over HTTPS to a Supabase research database hosted by the study team.

Protects privacy

Phone numbers and message content are SHA-256 hashed before storage. Raw content is never saved.
