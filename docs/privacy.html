Privacy Policy

Last updated: TODO update date · Data controller: University of Southampton

This privacy policy explains how the DETECTMiND research app collects, uses, and protects data from participants. By installing and using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who is responsible

The research study is led by Dr Anoop Kadan, Lecturer in Machine Learning at the University of Southampton (email: A.Kadan@soton.ac.uk). The University of Southampton is the data controller under UK GDPR.

2. What data is collected

Only the sensors enabled for your specific study cohort are collected. The full set of possible sensors:

SensorWhat is recordedPrivacy handling
App usageWhich apps you open, start/end time, durationPackage name only — no in-app content
NotificationsApp that posted a notification, time, title (if shown on lock screen)No notification body content stored
BatteryLevel, charging state, temperature, voltageNo identifiers
CallsDirection (incoming/outgoing/missed), duration, timePhone numbers are SHA-256 hashed before storage
SMSDirection, timeBoth contact number and message body are SHA-256 hashed; raw content never stored
LocationGPS coordinates, accuracy, speedCollected per study config — sampling rate varies
LightAmbient light sensor readingsNo identifiers
Screen stateOn / off / locked / unlocked eventsNo content
GesturesTaps, scrolls, long-presses — type and target app onlyNo on-screen content; OS-enforced via accessibility privacy flag
Survey responses (ESM)Your answers to short scheduled questionsLinked to your participant ID only

3. What is NOT collected

  • The content of messages, emails, or notifications
  • Phone numbers in plain text (only hashed)
  • What you type, see, or hear on the screen
  • Audio, video, photos, or microphone input
  • Browsing history or web content
  • Your real name or contact details (only your participant ID)

4. How data is stored and transmitted

  • All data is first stored locally on your device's encrypted app storage.
  • Data is uploaded over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) to a research database hosted by Supabase Inc. on EU servers.
  • Each upload is associated only with your study participant ID (a random UUID); your real identity is held separately by the research team in line with university data-management procedures.

5. Who can see the data

  • The research team at the University of Southampton.
  • The university's research ethics committee, if requested for audit purposes.
  • Data is not shared with advertisers, marketers, or any commercial third party.

6. How long data is kept

Data is retained for the duration of the study plus the period required by the University of Southampton's research data management policy (typically 10 years for published research outputs). After this period the data is securely destroyed.

7. Your rights under UK GDPR

  • Withdraw at any time, either from the app's Settings screen or by emailing the researcher.
  • Request a copy of the data linked to your participant ID.
  • Request deletion of your data from the research database.
  • Complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe your rights have been infringed.

8. Banking-app pause feature

Some banking apps refuse to open when an accessibility service is active. DETECTMiND detects when you launch a configured banking app and prompts you (via notification) to pause gesture collection. We do not collect or transmit any data about your banking activity itself.

9. Security

The Android app stores data locally on your device using Android's standard app-private storage, encrypted at rest by the OS. Network transmission uses TLS 1.2 or higher. The server uses row-level security to ensure only the research team can read participant data.

10. Children

This app is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. Contact

For any questions about how your data is handled, contact:

  • University of Southampton Data Protection Officer · data.protection@soton.ac.uk
